Blackjack

Blackjack, or 21, is a simple yet exciting card game where players aim to beat the dealer by getting as close to 21 as possible without going over. War

War is a straightforward casino game where players compete against the dealer by comparing a single card. Both the player and dealer are dealt one card, and the higher card wins. If the cards are equal, the player can either go to "war" or surrender. Going to war means drawing more cards to determine the winner, adding an extra layer of excitement to this simple game.

Jacks or Better

Jacks or Better is a classic video poker game where players aim to create a winning hand with at least a pair of jacks. After being dealt five cards, players can choose which cards to keep and which to discard in hopes of improving their hand. The game combines elements of strategy and luck, making it a favorite among both casual and experienced players looking for straightforward poker action.
